About US

WICKED WIPES is a brand new business run under the auspices of our founding company, Beatus Concepts Inc. Beatus (pronounced beahtus) means ”blessed” in Latin and as the founding members, we feel blessed to be working together as friends and business partners. We also feel blessed to be able to offer a high quality, healthful product to customers in the hospitality industry across Canada.

Our Mission

Our mission is to help people and spread goodwill via sharing useful and natural products that are good for people and the environment.
