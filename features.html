Design

The container pod is a two-chamber system. The cleansing solution is held in the outer chamber. The inner chamber contains a compressed cellulose cloth wipe. The two chambers are separated from each other until activated for use.Â 

When pressure is applied to the middle of the top of the pod, by thumb or finger, the container opens the valve at the bottom and the cleansing solution from the outer chamber reaches the cloth wipe in the inner chamber. The wipe then fully absorbs the cleanser and rises vertically to be removed easily and put to use.
